About us

Namaz Islamic Gifts is a platform that encourages and rewards Muslims for their dedication to performing regular Namaz. We understand the importance of consistency in prayer, and through our system, we recognize and celebrate those who remain committed to their faith. Our unique gifting program offers meaningful rewards for prayer dedication, motivating users to stay on track. Join us in strengthening your relationship with Allah (SWT) while being part of a supportive, like-minded community. We believe that prayer is not only a spiritual practice but a powerful way to build discipline, peace, and gratitude in everyday life. At Namaz Islamic Gifts, we aim to make each prayer more fulfilling by offering tangible rewards.

Vision

Our vision is to inspire Muslims worldwide to make prayer a regular, celebrated practice. We aim to foster a global community that embraces consistency in Namaz and encourages individuals to earn rewards through their devotion to faith.

Mission

Our mission is to reward and motivate individuals to stay committed to their prayers. We aim to create a supportive community, providing meaningful rewards that encourage users to maintain their prayer habits and deepen their connection with Allah (SWT).

What We Offer

We encourage a consistent Namaz routine and the reading of the Quran.
